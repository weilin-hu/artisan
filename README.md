# Welcome to **artisan**!

My love for art and feelings of defeat when my art piece does not look the way I want it to led me to developing this web application. On release, my vision is for this site to help developing artists not only share their work and progress with others (through what I call 'artifacts' and sketchbooks), but also overcome creative blocks with finding inspiration and motivation to create new, exciting pieces. 

This is my first large personal project, and I want to make it the best it can be. If you have suggestions or concerns, I would love to hear your thoughts! You can most conveniently reach me at `weilinhu@wharton.upenn.edu`.

To see my design progress so far, please visit the `pages` folder for images. I will hopefully deploy a 'fuller' app by end of this year!

Enjoy! :)
